21. Asmodeus - Succubus room

You unlock the Asmodeus room and enter, it is a luxurious bedroom with lit candles and a flowered aroma. In the middle of the room is a large plush bed, and beside it an oaken dressing table. Combing her long luscious hair is a young woman sitting in front of the dressing table, dressed in a dark green evening gown that matches her eyes. She appears surprised to see you, and introduces herself as Lilith.
